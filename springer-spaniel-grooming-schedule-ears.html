Ear Grooming Facts That Boost Your Confidence

Here are springer spaniel grooming schedule ears facts that replace doubt with knowledge:

  1. Floppy ears = higher infection risk. Their heavy, pendulous ears limit airflow. A consistent schedule reduces infections by up to 70%.
  2. Plucking is not cruel. Loose hair inside the ear canal traps wax and moisture. Gentle plucking (or using hemostats) actually reduces irritation.
  3. Smell is your early alert. A yeasty or sour odour means you need to adjust your schedule or see your vet.
  4. Your dog’s ears are unique. Some springers produce more wax; some have drier canals. Tailor your schedule to your dog — that’s not doubt, that’s expertise.
